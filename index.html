About me

Having worked for years as a designer for the event and entertainment industry I developed many entrepreneurial qualities and a broad range of soft skills. I adapt rapidly to diverse teams and projects. I love to spark passion and motivate people around me. I am a creative but well-structured person and an honest, but empathetic communicator.
I have a reliable design process and deep attention to the clients' needs. These help me to stay calm under pressure and deliver quality results with respect to deadlines. Having conducted longterm projects, I know how and when to focus on details, without losing sight of the overall picture.
I graduated from the UX/UI Design Bootcamp in April 2020 to adapt my design skills to the demands of the digital industry. I believe that an inclusive & user-centered approach can be profitable for any business. I am looking for positions within diverse teams of experts whom I can learn from and share my own expertise with.
I would like to take responsibility for setting the tone and visual language of digital products and immersive experiences. I have always been a curious adopter of new technologies and trends, so I am especially interested in working within the Saas industry and the AR (MR) environment to create innovative interactions and tools for brands and institutions.
